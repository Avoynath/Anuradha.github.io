OUR COMMUNITY

Katha’r’Kotha is a community, more-like a virtual platform where devout followers of the craft come together to share their ideas, knowledge and information about the craft. Therefore, benefiting the students who are specifically enthusiastic about Kantha and look forward to find grounds of practising in this field. The community builds a bridge between the craft clusters and the students sitting in different corners of the country enabling them to understand the craft better without visiting the cluster in person. A one-to one chat with the master artisans with the moderators directing the conversation, conclude in extensive feasibility to know the craft better.
